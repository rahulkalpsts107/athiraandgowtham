Transportation Information

‚úàÔ∏è From Kempegowda International Airport (KIA)

  • Airport Bus (BMTC Vayu Vajra): Take the KIA-5 bus route to Marathahalli, then use a ride-sharing service to the venue (approx. 15 min). Buses run every 30 minutes.
  • Airport Taxi: Direct taxis available at the airport, approx. 35-45 minutes to venue (depending on traffic).
  • App-based Cabs: Uber and Ola are readily available from the airport.

üöÜ Nearby Train Stations

  • Krishnarajapuram Railway Station: 8 km from venue. Convenient for guests from Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
  • Bengaluru Cantonment: 12 km from venue. Serves many routes from Kerala.
  • KSR Bengaluru (Majestic): 16 km from venue. Main railway station with connections to all major cities.

üöï Local Transportation

  • Metro: Nearest stations are Baiyappanahalli (Purple Line) and Indiranagar (Purple Line), both requiring a 15-20 min cab ride to venue.
  • Bus: BMTC buses connect all parts of the city. Route numbers 335E, 500K stop near Marathahalli Bridge (2 km from venue).
  • Auto Rickshaws: Available throughout the city, but use meter or negotiate fare before starting journey.
  • Rideshare: Uber and Ola provide convenient and affordable options to reach the venue.
  • Namma Yatri: Bengaluru's local auto-booking app that offers fair prices and direct payment to drivers. Great for shorter trips in the city.

Note: Traffic in Bengaluru can be unpredictable. We recommend adding 30-45 minutes buffer time to your travel plans, especially during peak hours (9-11 AM and 5-8 PM).
